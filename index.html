Every field validates against real Airbus FCOM data formats. V1≤VR≤V2 cross-checks, FLAPS/THS format enforcement, FL range limits — FORMAT ERROR and OUT OF RANGE just like the real box.

Interactive Tool
The MCDU

Use it directly in the browser. On a tablet it feels almost identical to the real thing. Plug in your SimBrief ID below the unit and hit UPLINK to load your flight plan.

INIT Enter FROM/TO — ICAO pair, flight number, cost index and cruise FL. Links to WIND page for wind data entry.
PERF Six phase performance — cycle with <PREV / NEXT> on row 6. All V-speeds cross-validated.
F-PLN Route from SimBrief — paginated waypoint list. Access DEPARTURE, ARRIVAL and HOLD from the F-PLN page.
ATSU AOC datalink — press L1 on AOC MENU to uplink OFP direct from SimBrief. L2 fetches live METAR for your departure airport.
PROG Live flight data — shows live FOB, speeds, AP targets when PC companion is connected. Page 2 for cruise level management.
BRT Brightness control — clickable bar or L4/L5 buttons to increase or decrease screen brightness.

Getting Started
How to use it
01
Enter your SimBrief ID

Type your SimBrief username or numeric pilot ID into the datalink panel beneath the MCDU and press UPLINK. Your full OFP loads instantly.

02
Check the INIT page

Press INIT to see your route, flight number, cost index and cruise FL all populated from SimBrief. Press INIT 2 for ZFW and fuel entries.

03
Set PERF data

Press PERF and cycle through TO, CLB, CRZ, DES, APPR and GA pages with the <PREV / NEXT> buttons. Enter V-speeds, FLEX temp and landing data.

04
Review F-PLN

Press F-PLN to scroll through your SimBrief route waypoints. Access DEPARTURE for runway selection, ARRIVAL for approach type, and HOLD for a holding pattern.

05
Monitor via PROG

Use the PROG page during flight. With the PC companion app running on MSFS 2020/2024, it shows live FOB, speeds and autopilot targets in real time.

06
ATSU for weather

Go to ATSU → AOC MENU → WX REQUEST to fetch a live METAR for your departure airport direct from Aviation Weather.

PC Users — MSFS 2020 / 2024
Live Sim Connection

Running MSFS on a PC? Download the companion app and get live sim data — real fuel burn, autopilot targets, N1 and more — on your phone or tablet while flying.

01 Download MCDU-Companion.exe from the GitHub releases page
02 Double-click to run — it connects to MSFS automatically
03 On the MCDU site, enter your PC's local IP (e.g. 192.168.1.50) in Settings and connect
04 The green dot in the MCDU title bar lights up — you're live

Xbox / console users: SimBrief uplink works fully without the companion app.
